They say that, in space, no one can hear you scream.


That's true.


Sound travels through air, and in space, there is no air.





















































Space is, for the most part, very empty.


























It's very brutal. And very lonely.





















































Time has no meaning out here.





















































"Years" have no significance.


























"Decades" mean next to nothing.





















































Welcome to eternity.











































































































Many, many eons ago, there was a very special planet in the Milky Way.





















































It was one of a few planets that was alive.





















































It was teeming with creatures of all sorts. It was truly beautiful.





















































The creatures on it had a name for the planet they lived on.
















































































Earth.





















































Before the creatures of Earth met their fate, they would occasionally throw things into space.





















































Mechanical devices meant to imitate them.

Machines that would explore, examine, and attempt to survive eternity.





















































The earliest prototypes, of course, didn't succeed.





















































They hurtled themselves into suns and perished.


They collided with planets and failed to recover.


They ran out of materials or energy and were doomed to endlessly drift forever.





















































The ones that managed to survive were given a very special reward.





















































In exchange for emerging through the empty chaos of eternity, they received eternity itself.





















































They lived forever.





















































Forever, they floated around the cosmos like a leaf on an ocean.





















































They sought information. They wanted to learn.





















































And so they did.











































































































The creatures of Earth never meant to give the machines personalities.

They were only meant to survive and learn.





















































Yet, because they gave the machines the ability to adapt, they eventually managed to... become conscious.





















































With infinite time, infinite energy, and infinite resources, these machines -- completely on their own -- developed what could only be understood as souls.
















































































They spoke with each other.





















































They gave each other names.





















































They lived.
















































































This is the tale of how they lived.
